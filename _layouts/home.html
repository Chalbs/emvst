Our Story

We're Alex and Matt. Two friends who are passionate about technology and sharing our knowledge with others. In 2013 we heard about this thing call Bitcoin. Like many of you, we thought it sounded interesting but it might just be a passing fad. We paid little attention to it at the time. Fast forward to early 2017 when Bitcoin and other digital currenies started making headlines on major news outlets like CNN. After the market skyrocketed, we knew we wanted to dive deeper into the techoology behind digital currencies. It turns out that there's a lot going on behind the scenes. We're not just dealing with "virtual money" here. The technology powering many of the most popular digital currencies actually has amazing opportunity to transform industries from finance to healthcare.
